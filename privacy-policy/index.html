Privacy Policy

At Dentoamigo, your privacy is extremely important to us. This policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our website, fill out forms, or use our services.
By using our website, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our website or communicate with us, we may collect the following types of personal and non-personal data:

  1. Personal Information:

    • Full name
    • Contact number
    • Email address
    • Location (via IP address)
    • Dental concerns/messages submitted through forms

  2. Non-Personal Information:

    • Browser type
    • Device type
    • Referral source
    • Date & time of visit
    • Cookies and session tracking data.

2. How We Collect Your Data

We collect data when you:

  • Submit a contact or appointment form
  • Subscribe to any newsletter or updates
  • Browse the website and accept cookies
  • Interact with our social media plugins or WhatsApp button

3. How We Use Your Information

The data collected is used for the following purposes:

  • To respond to appointment bookings and contact queries
  • To personalize your user experience
  • To send appointment reminders and updates (if opted in)
  • To improve website performance, content, and services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations

4. Cookie Policy

Our website uses cookies to collect certain browsing data to improve your user experience. Cookies may track:

  • Session time
  • Pages visited
  • Return visits
  • IP address

5. Data Storage & Security

We store personal data on secure servers and take industry-standard measures to protect your information, including:

  • Any loss or damage due to HTTPS encryption
  • Technical issues, errors, Secure hosting platforms
  • User misinterpretation of Restricted access to data
  • User misinterpretation of Regular security updates

6. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to others.
However, your data may be shared

  • With trusted third-party providers (e.g., hosting, email, analytics) who assist in running our website
  • When required by law or to protect rights, safety, and security

7. Third-Party Links

We may update or modify these Our website may include links to third-party websites (e.g., Google Maps, Instagram, Facebook).
We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these websites. Users are advised to review their privacy policies separately.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct any inaccuracies in your data
  • Request data deletion (as per applicable law)
  • Withdraw consent to communication

To exercise any of the above rights, contact us at info@dentoamigo.com

9. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
If you believe we have inadvertently collected data from a child, please contact us for immediate removal.

10. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update or revise this privacy policy at any time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
