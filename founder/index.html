Meet the Founder

Dr. Ravina Duhan

Founder & Director

Creating Smiles Since 2007

Hello,

Professionally, I founded my dental practice in Dwarka, India with a vision to change how people feel about visiting the dentist and to contribute to the empowerment of women in dentistry. Let’s face it—many people still feel anxious about dental visits, and with increasing commercialization in the industry, the personal connection between patient and doctor often gets lost.

At my clinic, the focus is on creating a comfort-centric experience that puts the patient first. I’ve worked to build a space that feels friendly, welcoming, and familiar, where patients can receive care without fear or stress. Each aspect of the clinic reflects the warmth and culture of the Dwarka community.

We are constantly innovating. My goal has always been to stay at the forefront of general and cosmetic dentistry, using advanced technologies to provide the highest standard of care. I believe in constantly evolving—whether it’s adopting the latest in dental tech or improving the overall patient journey.

But for me, dentistry is more than just creating beautiful smiles. It’s about empowering people—helping patients feel confident and motivated to take better care of themselves. It’s also about empowering my team, creating a work environment that is inclusive, inspiring, and supportive.

Since starting in 2007, I’ve had the privilege of treating thousands of patients, and I continue to be passionate about making a positive impact through every smile we restore.

— Dr. Ravina Duhan
