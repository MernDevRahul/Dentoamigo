Dental Implants at Dentoamigo – Restore Your Smile with Confidence

What Are Dental Implants?

The dental implant procedure serves as a long-term and naturally resembling system to replace lost teeth. Dentoamigo delivers state-of-the-art implant procedures that restores both functionality and visual appeal to create strong permanent smiles.

A dental implant system includes a titanium root base, which functions as an artificial tooth anchor and combines with a connecting piece that links to an individual-specific crown reconstruction. Dental implants achieve unmatched stability through their jawbone anchoring since they differ from dentures and bridges as fixed components.

The ideal treatment for people with one or more missing teeth consists of dental implants that simultaneously enhance appearance along with improving speech abilities, and enabling proper chewing function.

Benefits of Dental Implants

  • Permanent & Durable – Implants can last a lifetime with proper care.
  • Natural Look & Feel – Designed to match your real teeth seamlessly.
  • Restores Chewing Ability – Eat your favorite foods without restrictions.
  • Prevents Bone Loss – Stimulates the jawbone to maintain facial structure.
  • No Damage to Nearby Teeth – Unlike dental bridges, implants do not affect adjacent teeth.

Who Can Get Dental Implants?

  • Have one or more missing teeth due to decay, injury, or extractions
  • Have sufficient jawbone density to support the implant
  • Maintain good oral hygiene and overall health
  • Want a long-term alternative to dentures or bridges

If you’re unsure whether dental implants are right for you, our expert team at Dentoamigo will evaluate your oral health and recommend the best treatment plan.

Our Dental Implant Procedure

  1. Consultation & Evaluation – Your oral condition will be examined then X-ray images will be taken along with an evaluation of your suitability for implant placement.
  2. Implant Placement – Surgeons use a titanium implant to position it directly into the jawbone before applying it as the base for the new tooth.
  3. Healing & Osseointegration – Time and procedures enable the implant to unite with jaw bone tissue, leading to a solid foundation.
  4. Abutment & Crown Placement – After healing is finished, a personal crown connects to the implant to finish your new smile.

The procedure provides enduring safety alongside effectiveness which results in comfortable functionality and natural-looking outcomes.

Post-Treatment Care & Maintenance

  • Daily practice of proper oral hygiene habits includes brushing teeth along with flossing.
  • Strengthened implants must avoid exposure to solid foods since they can cause damage.
  • Attend regular dental checkups for monitoring and professional cleaning.
  • You should cease smoking because it helps with healing and increases the success rate of implants.
